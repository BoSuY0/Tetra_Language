# P17.4 Safe-Semantics Closure Design

## Scope

Close the P17.4 master-plan row "Make sure these flags do not change safe
semantics" as evidence-only validation.

This closure does not add public PGO/profile/LTO/target-cpu flags, optimizer
rewrites, target-specific codegen, LTO consumers, or performance claims.

## Design

- Keep `PGOLTOTargetCPUCoverage()` as the P17.4 coverage matrix.
- Add `ValidatePGOLTOTargetCPUSafeSemanticsClosure()` to reject fake completion
  or fake semantic-changing claims in that matrix.
- Add `PGOLTOTargetCPUSafeSemanticsClosure()` as a compact evidence record for
  the final P17.4 safe-semantics row.
- Use negative guards for:
  - semantic-changing coverage rows;
  - incomplete or missing-fact rows;
  - fake profile-format optimizer input;
  - profile-guided rewrite policy;
  - target-specific optimization evidence;
  - LTO codegen/linker consumers.
